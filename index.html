Why OARN?

Built from the ground up for decentralization, privacy, and open science

No Hardcoded Values

All infrastructure discovered dynamically via ENS and DHT. No central points of failure.

Privacy by Default

Optional Tor routing, traffic padding, and ephemeral addresses. Your research stays private.

Built on Arbitrum

Low fees, fast finality, Ethereum security. Perfect for microtransactions.

Framework Agnostic

Support for ONNX, PyTorch, and more. Run any model on any compatible node.

Community Governed

GOV token holders decide protocol upgrades, fee structures, and treasury allocation.

Earn COMP Rewards

Run a compute node and earn COMP tokens for every task you complete.

How It Works

Three simple steps to decentralized AI compute

1

Submit Task

Upload your model and input data to IPFS. Submit a task with your requirements and reward.

2

Node Executes

A compute node with matching capabilities claims your task and runs inference.

3

Get Results

Results are uploaded to IPFS. The node receives COMP rewards automatically.

Dual Token Model

Designed for sustainability and fair value distribution

GOV Governance Token
  • Supply: 100M (Fixed)
  • Purpose: Voting, staking for providers
  • Distribution:
    • 40% Early contributors
    • 30% Public sale
    • 20% DAO treasury
    • 10% Core team (4yr vest)
COMP Compute Token
  • Supply: Inflationary (decreasing)
  • Purpose: Task rewards, payments
  • Emission:
    • Year 1: 100M
    • Year 2: 80M (-20%)
    • Year 3: 64M (-20%)
    • Optional 2% burn on transfer
