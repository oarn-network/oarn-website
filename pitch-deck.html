The Problem

Centralized Control

AWS, Google, Azure control 90% of AI compute. Single points of failure, censorship risk.

Unaffordable Access

GPU costs $3-5/hour. Small researchers priced out. Innovation concentrated in Big Tech.

No Verification

Cloud providers can return wrong results. No way to verify AI outputs are correct.

Privacy Concerns

Sensitive research data sent to centralized servers. IP theft, surveillance risks.

Idle GPU Capacity

Millions of consumer GPUs sit idle. No marketplace to monetize spare compute.

Vendor Lock-in

Proprietary APIs, formats, workflows. Switching costs keep users trapped.

Our Solution

A decentralized network where anyone can provide AI compute and get verified results through multi-node consensus.

Multi-Node Consensus

3+ nodes execute each task. Results compared on-chain. Impossible to cheat.

ONNX Runtime

Industry-standard ML framework. Any model, any hardware. No vendor lock-in.

Built on Arbitrum

Low fees ($0.01/tx), fast finality (250ms), Ethereum security.
